CATALYSIS 2K24

Catalysis 2k24 is set to be an enriching symposium event spanning from July 19 to 21, offering a diverse range of activities catering to both technical and non-technical enthusiasts. At its core, the symposium promises stimulating discussions and presentations on cutting-edge catalysis research, providing a platform for scholars, researchers, and industry professionals to exchange ideas and explore the latest advancements in the field. Alongside the technical sessions, attendees can engage in hands-on workshops designed to deepen their understanding of catalytic processes and techniques. Moreover, embracing a holistic approach, Catalysis 2k24 integrates cultural events, fostering networking opportunities in a relaxed and vibrant atmosphere. From art exhibitions to cultural performances, participants will have the chance to unwind and connect with peers, enhancing both their professional and personal experiences at the symposium.
